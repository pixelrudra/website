Vision

I'm Rudrapratap Therokar, a passionate game developer specializing in pixel art and interactive storytelling. With over 8 years of experience in the indie game scene, I create immersive worlds that blend retro aesthetics with modern gameplay mechanics.

My journey began when I first discovered pixel art animation as a teenager, fascinated by how simple pixels could convey complex emotions and narratives. This passion led me to pursue game development professionally, where I've honed my skills across multiple game engines and programming languages.

When I'm not coding, you can find me sketching new character designs, studying classic game mechanics, or mentoring aspiring developers in online communities.
